📖 Glossary / How to read this table

Half-life (T½)

Time for half the atoms to decay. Ranges from femtoseconds to billions of years.

Specific Activity

Decays per second per gram (Bq/g). Formula: A = ln(2)·Nₐ/(T½·M). Click 📋 to copy.

Decay modes

α emits ⁴He. β⁻ neutron→proton. β⁺/EC proton→neutron. IT isomeric transition. SF spontaneous fission.

Gamma (γ) radiation

Photons emitted per 100 parent decays. Σ intensity = total per decay. Total keV/decay = total photon energy released.

Isomers (m)

Long-lived excited nuclear states (e.g. ⁹⁹ᵐTc). Key in nuclear medicine — IT decay emits useful γ.

Medical symbols

◎ Diagnostic (PET/SPECT) · ⚡ Therapy · ☯ Theranostic. ✓ = established clinical use · … = in development.
