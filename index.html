Data Sources & Methodology

IAEA Live Chart of Nuclides
Ground-state half-lives, decay modes, branching ratios, atomic masses and Q-values for 3,383 nuclides (Z = 1–118), retrieved from the IAEA Nuclear Data Section API, which serves ENSDF/NUBASE-evaluated data.
www-nds.iaea.org/relnsd
Selected metastable isomers
Excitation energies, isomeric half-lives and branching (e.g. Tc-99m, Co-60m, Am-242m) from the IAEA nuclear level scheme database (ENSDF evaluations).
Cross-reference institutions
Underlying ENSDF evaluations are produced collaboratively by national labs and centres including NNDC (Brookhaven), IAEA-NDS, and the JRC-Geel (formerly JRC-ITU).
Specific activity is derived (not directly tabulated) using A = ln(2)·NA / (T½ · M), the standard relation used by NNDC/ORNL isotope reference sheets, where NA = 6.02214076×10²³ mol⁻¹. Decay energies are ground-state Q-values; for isomers, isomeric-transition energy equals the level excitation energy. Values shown in scientific notation. This tool is for educational reference — for regulatory or clinical use, consult primary IAEA/ENSDF evaluations directly.
